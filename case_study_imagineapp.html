Project Overview

ImagineAPP is a creator-focused platform launched in 2022, dedicated to empowering artists in the AIGC (AI-generated content) space. The platform offers a seamless experience for image and video generation—and most notably—an exclusive Auto AI Music Video Maker.

As the sole UI/UX designer on the team, I was responsible for the entire end-to-end design process. This included task flow design, wireframing, prototyping, high-fidelity UI, usability testing, and post-launch iterations.

Case Study: Auto AI Music Video Maker

1. Problem

Despite the rise of AIGC tools, there's a significant usability gap for creators—especially musicians and indie artists—who lack technical backgrounds. Creating an AI-generated music video typically takes days and requires multiple tools, manual prompt tuning, and video editing knowledge.

Our user survey revealed:

  • Creating a single AI music video takes ~1 week of human effort.
  • Artists and labels often have large music catalogs in need of video content.
  • The global music market was valued at $10.69B in 2022, with a projected CAGR of 7.20% (2022–2029)—a massive opportunity for scalable, automated video creation.

2. Solution

We developed an all-in-one AI music video maker that empowers artists to generate music videos by simply describing what they want—no technical skills required. Our tool bridges multiple AI models and complex workflows into one cohesive, intuitive, and effective user experience.
